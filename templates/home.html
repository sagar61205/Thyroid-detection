Types of thyroid related issues

Compensated hypothyroid

Compensated hypothyroidism occurs when the pituitary gland has to overwork the thyroid gland to keep the level of thyroid hormones normal in the blood.

Primary hypothyroid

Primary hypothyroidism is defined as low levels of blood thyroid hormone due to destruction of the thyroid gland.

Secondary hypothyroid

Secondary hypothyroidism is a rare disorder that describes an underactive pituitary gland causing an underactive thyroid.
